About me

Hello! My name is Elena. I am a professional photographer, and I do this with love and great interest. I experience each shoot with you, and sometimes it’s hard to say who enjoys it more – you or me. My favourite genres are portrait photography in the open air, whether in the city or in nature, as well as studio art portrait photography.

Besides the shooting itself, an important aspect for me is the processing of the photos. I constantly follow the works of renowned photographers, always learning and taking individual lessons from professionals. New methods and processing techniques help to create beautiful and unusual images that keep up with modern trends. However, every photographer has their own unique vision, so their works will always differ, much like different artists cannot paint the same landscape in the same way.

The most pleasant moments for me are when people like my work. Seeing how satisfied they are with the results of our joint efforts and receiving positive feedback is a real reward. I put my soul into every frame, and I hope you can feel that. As they say, everything made by human hands carries a piece of warmth and energy, whether it’s a clay pot or a photograph.

My distinctive vision forms a unique style, allowing me to create unusual and diverse photographs. Experience and ease in communication help to capture sincere emotions, making reportage lively and posed shots natural. I personally edit each photograph, infusing it with my vision and skill.

I always strive for an individual approach. While maintaining my style, I reflect in my shots the story, character, and emotions of people, as every person is unique. I am always ready to listen to your wishes and offer my ideas that best match your preferences.

Which of my photographs is my favourite? The one I am going to take tomorrow, as Imogen Cunningham once said.
